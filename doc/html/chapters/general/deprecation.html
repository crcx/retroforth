Deprecation Policy

As Retro evolves, some words will become obsolete and no longer be needed. In each release, these will be marked as deprecated in the glossary. Any deprecated words will be removed in the next quarterly release.

E.g., if 2020.1 had deprecated words, these would be removed in the 2020.4 release. Any words made deprecated in between 2020.1 and 2020.4 would be removed in the 2020.7 release.
